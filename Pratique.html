The best resto in the street

From atmosphere to cuisine, Serrano Vista Cafe provides unique dishes that will bring back the nostalgia of being in en grove in bloom. Southern California classics using local, fresh produce and organic grass-fed beeffrom nearby ranchers delight your senses and complement anyone's flavor profile.

The Grove Express offers fresh meals and light bites without the wait Enjoy prepared breakfast, lunch, or a flavorful en rée daily from 8am-11pm.Located next to Serrano Vista Café, the outlet is your go-to for all things convenient.

Kick up your feet in rooms designed with you in mind. We’ve curated each one to feel like home with amenities you’ll love to explore over and over again from cozy double queens to luxurious suites. Each room features beautiful furniture & décor inspired by the native landscape. Whatever the occasion, we’ve got you covered.
